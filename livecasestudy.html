Case Study

project overview

Assam Green Tea provides green tea all over the world. They need a responsive website so that they can reach out to more and more people thorough the internet on desktop and mobile devices

Business Goals

  • Search visibility.
  • Build trust with buyers, supporter and customers.
  • Accessibility and user-experience.
  • Higher conversion rate.
  • Increase website site traffic 50% and sales 20% in years 1-5 by investing in aggressive Web search optimization and marketing

Usability Goals

  • Create a nice-looking, easy-to-use website as it provides the assurance that the company is growing and on top of the latest trends and technologies.
  • Make responsive website and provide a seamless experience across all devices.
  • User Friendly and easy to navigate.
  • Reflect the latest products and marketing strategy.
  • Update good content to set apart from the competition and deliver right message to users.
